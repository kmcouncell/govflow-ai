# General Records Schedule — Program Administration *(excerpt, synthetic)*

**Schedule ID:** GRS 1.1 — EXCERPT ONLY  
**Agency:** National Archives and Records Administration (fictional excerpt for training)

## Series: Correspondence and internal memoranda

| Item | Description | Retention | Disposition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1a | Transitory email and instant messages not containing substantive policy decisions | Destroy when 90 days old | Temporary |
| 1b | Substantive policy memoranda and decision logs | 6 years after superseded | Permanent after agency review |
| 1c | Interagency agreement drafts that were not executed | 3 years after abandonment | Temporary |

## Electronic records

Electronic copies follow the retention of the **record copy** (usually the document management system version). Convenience copies in personal mailboxes must not replace the record repository.

## Cutoff and transfer

Cutoff occurs at the end of the **fiscal year** in which the case file closes. Agencies must prepare SF 115 metadata before transfer to NARA-approved repositories.
