# Equal Employment Opportunity — Anti-harassment program directive *(synthetic sample)*

**EEO-DIR-2023-04**

## Policy statement

Harassment based on race, color, religion, sex (including pregnancy, sexual orientation, and gender identity), national origin, age (40 or older), disability, or genetic information is prohibited in all aspects of employment.

## Reporting pathways

Employees may report concerns to:

1. Their immediate supervisor (unless the supervisor is the subject of the report).  
2. The agency EEO office within **45 calendar days** of the alleged incident for formal EEO counseling.  
3. The agency inspector general hotline for fraud, waste, abuse, or retaliation concerns.

## Supervisor obligations

Supervisors who receive a report must **promptly** notify the EEO office, avoid prejudgment, and take interim measures when necessary to prevent retaliation.

## Records

Investigative files are maintained separately from performance files and accessed only on a need-to-know basis.
