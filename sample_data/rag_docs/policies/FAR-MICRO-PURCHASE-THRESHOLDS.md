# Micro-purchase and simplified acquisition thresholds — desk guide *(synthetic)*

**Audience:** Contracting officers and purchase card holders  
**References:** FAR Part 13 (simplified acquisition procedures) — illustrative only

## Micro-purchase cardholder

Purchases at or below the micro-purchase threshold may be awarded without soliciting competitive quotations if the price is **reasonable**. Cardholders must:

- Split requirements only when legitimately separate; **artificial splitting** to avoid competition thresholds is prohibited.
- Document the determination of price reasonableness (catalog price, market survey, or prior paid invoice).
- Route software purchases through the enterprise software review board when applicable.

## Competition requirements

When the anticipated value exceeds the micro-purchase threshold, follow small purchase procedures including obtaining **quotes from at least three** sources when practicable, or documenting why fewer quotes were used.

## Environmental and socio-economic policies

Orders must comply with applicable **Energy Star**, recycled content, and small business set-aside rules when thresholds in FAR Part 23 are met.
