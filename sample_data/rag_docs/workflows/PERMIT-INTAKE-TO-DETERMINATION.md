# Workflow example — Environmental permit intake to determination *(synthetic)*

## Actors

- Applicant (external)  
- Intake clerk  
- Technical reviewer  
- Supervisor (signing official)

## Steps

1. **Intake (Day 0–2).** Applicant submits form, fee, and attachments via the portal. Intake clerk validates completeness and assigns a tracking number.
2. **Completeness review (Day 3–7).** Technical reviewer requests missing studies or corrections. Clock pauses when a **deficiency notice** is issued.
3. **Technical analysis (Day 8–45).** Reviewer evaluates impact models, public comment summary, and mitigation measures.
4. **Decision draft (Day 46–55).** Supervisor reviews draft determination (approve, approve with conditions, or deny).
5. **Publication.** Final decision posted to the public register; applicant receives certified notice.

## SLAs

Internal target: **90%** of complete applications reach a draft decision within 60 calendar days. Timers exclude applicant-caused delays.
