# Privacy — PII minimization handbook excerpt *(synthetic)*

## Collection limitation

Collect only the **minimum** PII necessary to fulfill a stated purpose compatible with the **Privacy Act** system of records notice (SORN) or equivalent authority.

## Field-level guidance

- Prefer **agency-issued identifiers** over Social Security numbers in new workflows.  
- Mask or tokenize account numbers in user interfaces; display last four digits only when business need exists.  
- Do not embed PII in URLs, query strings, or client-side analytics events.

## Retention

PII must be disposed of according to the **approved retention schedule**. Early deletion is permitted when no legal hold applies.

## Breach readiness

Maintain a current **roster** of systems containing PII, data flows to subcontractors, and encryption status to support 24-hour internal escalation timelines.
